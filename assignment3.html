HOW TO PREPARE JOLLOF RICE

INGREDIENTS
  1. Cups of rice long grain
  2. Cup of groundnut oil
  3. Tomatoes
  4. Tomato paste
  5. Blended pepper
  6. Thyme
  7. Curry powder
  8. Seasoning cubes
  9. Fried chicken
  10. onions
  11. bay leaves
  12. White pepper [optional]

STEPS
    • The first step is laying the foundation by building up the flavour
    • sauteeing the onions which should about 3 to 5 minutes
    • The next thing is to stir in the tomato paste
      • This adds a deep and rich tomato flavour
    • Add the blended pepper and stir to mix
      • Usually,there is until it becomes really thick and the oil literally flows on it.
    • Add the thyme,curry,white pepper[if you wish] and the seasoning cubes
      • Adjust the seasoning at this point if there is a need to.
    • once the flavour is on point,then add the rice
      • Make sure to srir the rice properly until the each grains of rice is covered with the sauce
    • Now pour enough water in the pot and add the chicken stock
    • Give it a brief stir and cover it up with a tight fitting lid
      • If the lid is not fitting enough,cover it with a foil paper before covering it with the lid
      This is because Jollof rice needs a lot steam in order to turn out well
    • once the rice comes to a boil,reduce the heat to medium low and continue to cook until the rice is done about 20 to 25 minutes
    • Serve with fried chicken
